What is the problem you are trying to solve?

Take a stab at framing it as a design question.

Now state the ultimate impact you are trying to have.

What are some possible solutions to your problem? Think broadly. It's fine to start a project with a hunch or two, but make sure you allow for suprising outcomes.

Finall, write down some of the context and constraints that you're facing. They could be geographic, technological, time-based, or have to do with the population you are trying to reach.

Does your original question need a tweak? Try it again.
