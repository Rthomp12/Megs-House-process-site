The benifits of this design challenge

What is the problem you are trying to solve?

The problem I am trying to solve, is helping women and children struggling with domestic violence.

Take a stab at framing it as a design question.

How can Meg's House redesign ensure women and children feel safe and empowered during their strugggles?

Now state the ultimate impact you are trying to have.

The ultimate impact I am trying to have on these women is to make sure they feel included and know that they can trust this company and its values.

What are some possible solutions to your problem? Think broadly. It's fine to start a project with a hunch or two, but make sure you allow for suprising outcomes.
